Ad Recreation: American Cruise Lines

The Cruise Lines project was a collaborative effort that involved extensive research, creative brainstorming, and iterative design. We explored various layouts and concepts, refining our ideas to create a visually captivating advertisement. Feedback and iteration were key in fine-tuning the design, ensuring it effectively communicated the brand's essence. The final recreation featured a striking West Coast silhouette and iconic landmarks, inviting viewers to embark on a journey along America's coastline. Through teamwork and attention to detail, we brought the brand's vision to life, inspiring wanderlust and captivating audiences.
